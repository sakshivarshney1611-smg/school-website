About Aligarh Muslim University

Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) is a central university located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was originally established by Sir Syed Ahmad Khan as the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College in 1875, which later became AMU in 1920.

AMU offers more than 300 courses in traditional and modern branches of education. It has students from all over India and even abroad, making it a truly diverse university.
